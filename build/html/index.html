class bank_account.bank_account.BankAccount(account_number: int, client_number: int, balance: float, date_created: date)¶

Abstract base class for a bank account.

Stores account number, client number, balance, and creation date. Provides deposit, withdraw, and balance update methods. Subclasses must implement get_service_charges().

Also acts as a Subject in the Observer Pattern, allowing observers (e.g., Client) to receive notifications when significant account activities occur.

class bank_account.investment_account.InvestmentAccount(account_number: int, client_number: int, balance: float, date_created: date, management_fee: float)¶

Represents an investment account with a management fee.

Inherits from BankAccount and applies a management fee unless the account has existed for over 10 years, in which case the fee is waived.

get_service_charges() → float¶

Calculate the service charges for this investment account.

Returns:

float: Base service charge plus management fee if applicable.

Notes:

Accounts older than ten years are charged only the base fee. Accounts younger than ten years include the management fee in addition to the base service charge.

class bank_account.saving_account.SavingAccount(account_number: int, client_number: int, balance: float, date_created: date, minimum_balance: float)¶

Represents a savings account with a minimum balance requirement.

Inherits from BankAccount and applies higher service charges if the balance falls below the minimum balance. The calculation is delegated to MinimumBalanceStrategy.

get_service_charges() → float¶

Calculate the service charges for this savings account.

Returns:

float: Service charge calculated by MinimumBalanceStrategy.
